Full-Time Courses Offered

M Tech. Computer Science (Specialization in Computer and Information Science): 24 seats

Academic Eligibility for MTech(Computer and Information Science):
First class (60% marks) or percentage equivalent of GPA in B.E / B. Tech in any discipline or AMIETE / AMIE or M. Sc Physics with Electronics as special subject or M.Sc Mathematics. 2014 admission onwards

B Tech Degree in Computer Science and Engineering: 90 seats

Additional 5% students are allotted in each branch for B.Tech Degree Course from Kerala Engineering Entrance examination Rank list as per the Govt. of Kerala orders (Tuition Fee Waiver scheme for women, economically backward and physically handicapped meritorious students.) Lateral entry Admission to Second Year for Diploma holders - Additional 10% in Annual intake (Govt.& Management Seats in each branch )

Academic Eligibility for BTech:

Candidates who have passed Higher Secondary Examination, Kerala, or Examinations recognized as equivalent thereto, with 50 % marks in Mathematics separately, and 50 % marks in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry/Biotechnology/Computer Science /Biology put together are eligible for admission Allotment to 95% seats of the annual intake is made by Commissioner for Entrance examination (CEE), Government of Kerala from the rank list of Kerala Engineering Entrance examination. For Details of Kerala Engineering Entrance examination and allotment see the website : http://www.cee-kerala.org 5% seats are reserved for non-resident Indians (NRI seats). For NRI admission separate applications are invited by IHRD and rank list is prepared on the basis of qualifying examination and students are admitted from this rank list.
